Abstract Painting

Acrylic Paints : Acrylic paints are versatile and come in a wide array of colors. They can be used in various ways, including layering, pouring, and creating textures.
Oil Paints :Oil paints offer rich colors and the ability to blend smoothly. They're suitable for techniques like impasto (thick layering) and glazing (transparent layers).
Watercolors :Watercolors are translucent and allow for delicate washes of color. They're often used for abstract pieces with a softer, ethereal feel.
