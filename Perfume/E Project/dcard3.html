Product Details:

Sauvage Parfum is a highly concentrated interpretation of Sauvage, melding extreme freshness with warm ambery tones and an animal beauty that begs to come alive on the skin.

To compose Sauvage Parfum, Dior drew inspiration from a wild, unspoiled expanse beneath a blue-tinged night sky, as the intense odors of a crackling fire rise into the air. Its trail unfurls notes of mandarin, tonka bean and sandalwood.
